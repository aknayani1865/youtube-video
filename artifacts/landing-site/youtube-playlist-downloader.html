YouTube Playlist Downloader

Paste a playlist link or a YouTube watch link with a playlist parameter, review the playlist details, and choose a ZIP quality for supported videos.

Download all videos from a YouTube playlist

Many users paste a normal YouTube watch URL that includes both v= and list=. vidssave treats the playlist parameter as the important part, so a playlist link opens the playlist workflow instead of reducing the URL to one video.

The playlist downloader can show the playlist title, author, video count, thumbnails, and individual playlist items before a ZIP download begins. This helps users confirm that the correct playlist loaded before spending time processing many videos.

Playlist downloads can take longer than single video downloads because every video must be checked and packaged. Larger playlists, HD quality, and 4K choices can increase processing time and file size. For a faster result, choose 720p, 480p, 360p, or audio-only when those options fit your use case.
